Protect trans youth

Grassroot organizations you can donate to in order to support trans youth in Texas and fight the bigotry of Gov. Greg Abbott labeling gender-affirming care for trans kids as 'abuse'. Let's show them that love will prevail.

Trans folks support

  • Trans Texas

    Largest trans led, trans focused organization in the state of Texas furthering gender diverse equality in Texas through a racial justice lense with an intersectional approach.

  • Latina Trans Texas

    Community-based organization made up of trans women, organizing empowerment and leadership groups, providing temporary housing, and offering support and resources.

  • Saga

    Peer-to-peer support for the San Antonio transgender community, exploring issues and public policy involving gender identity.

  • Trans Pride Initiative

    Working to provide assistance and support for transgender and gender diverse persons in areas of healthcare, housing, employment, education, and public accommodations.

BIPOC trans folks support

  • Save Our Sisters United

    Empowering, connecting, and mobilizing trans people of color and women of ethnic minority through self and community empowerment, while better-educating society on anti-norms, stigma, gender biases, and trauma.

  • Black Trans Texas Connection

    Provides community and resources to black trans women experiencug

  • Black Trans Leadership of Austin

    Uses grant funding and donations to give direct aid and relief to folks in the QTBIPOC comunity in Central Texas.

Queer support

  • Allgo

    Building, educating, and mobilizing queer communities through cultural arts, wellness, and social justice programming.

  • Esperanza Center

    Community resources by and for San Antonians, founded by queer latinas

  • Fiesta Youth

    Events, trainings, and programming that aims to support queer youth and grow together as a community.

  • Thrive Youth Center

    Shelter for LGBTQ+ youth experiencing homelessness in San Antonio.

  • Abounding Prosperity

    Supporting Texans living with HIV, primarily black gay and bisexual men, cis women, trans women and all their families.

  • Tony's Place

    Org for Houson queer youth experiencing homelessness, housing insecutiy and other needs.

  • OutYouth

    Offers youth of all sexual orientations and gender identities a place where they are loved, acknowledged, and accepted for exactly who they are.

  • The Truth Project

    Healing and truth through storytelling for LGBTQ+ people in Houston
